PvP Proficieny

When engaging with an player in PvP you should figure out the following

  • How much poise your opponent has, and consequently how many attack it takes to stagger them etc
  • What utilities do they have? Magic? Pyromancy? 14 Dex? 16 Str? Miracles?
  • What is their ring setup? Are they HavelFap? Dual ring? FaP + Hornet? etc
  • How much latency do you have with your opponent?
  • Do they exhibit any patterns or weaknesses? Constant reverse rolls? Heavy locked strafing? Not holding block? Poor stamina management?
  • Can they counterswap faster than you?
  • Can they toggle escape?

Mindgames

Some common baits:

  • Movement baits, involves faking movement and direction to intice the opponent to defend against the perceived threat. This is often done on wakeup in an attempt to make the opponent react and move (ending their BS immunity).
  • Rolling attack baits

Backstabs

My main problem with backstabs was that I was afraid to commit to the unlocked backstab. If my opponent and I were strafing I had the tendency to roll away. Once you overcome this barrier your BS defence and offence improve.

Wall backstabs

Learn which BS options to use in the different situations is key

Backstab Option/InputProsCons
Standard R1 Backstab
  • Simple input
  • Results in a R1 if the backstab misses
Parry Backstab
  • Can be cancelled with a roll, backstep, stance change or toggle
  • The parry animation extends the BS window
  • Locks you in place for the duration of the parry animation
Kick Option Select Backstab
  • Missing the BS results in a kick which is often safer than an R1
  • Input can be tricky when unlocked
Toggle Option Select Backstab
  • The stored toggle will automatically toggle if you get staggered during the BS attempt
  • Not suitable if using a weapon buff
Pyro Option Select Backstab
  • Missing the BS results in a (presumably) Great Combustion cast

PvP Hotspots

The majority of meta level (120-135) PvP occurs in Oolacile Township, however, the Undead Burg and Darkroot are visited infrequently.

If you're not big into cheating (NoDead / Undead) then arena is a fun and quick way to get PvP with a friend.
