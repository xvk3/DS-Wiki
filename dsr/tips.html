PvP Proficieny

When engaging with an player in PvP you should figure out the following

  • How much poise your opponent has, and consequently how many attack it takes to stagger them.
  • What utilities do they have? Magic? Pyromancy? 14 Dex? 16 Str? Miracles?
  • What is their ring setup? Are they HavelFap? Dual ring? FaP + Hornet? etc
  • How much latency do you have with your opponent?
  • Do they exhibit any patterns or weaknesses? Constant reverse rolls? Heavy locked strafing? Not holding block? Poor stamina management? Repetitive similar chains attempts?
  • Can they counterswap faster than you?
  • Can they toggle escape?

Counterpicking

A weapon or armour swap performed in order to gain a matchup advantage against a armor or weapon setup. (Example, swapping to estoc+shield to get an advantage against chaos blade, or from chaos blade to gold tracer against estoc+shield. For armour, swapping to more resistant gear to lower damage: Dingy robe against moonlight greatsword) Rings can also be used to counter pick, such as hornet or DWGR, or even resistance rings like steel or stoneplate. (From the PvP Glossary)

Mindgames

Some common baits:

  • Failed BS Bait
  • Movement baits, involves faking movement and direction to intice the opponent to defend against the perceived threat. This is often done on wakeup in an attempt to make the opponent react and move (ending their BS immunity).
  • Rolling attack baits
  • Pyro Hand Bait
  • Controller Disconnect Bait
  • Bleed Bait

Backstabs

My main problem with backstabs was that I was afraid to commit to the unlocked backstab. If my opponent and I were strafing (towards each other) I had the tendency to roll away. Once you overcome this mental barrier your BS defence and offence will improve.

Wall backstabs - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Oh1Xe8ZOJBs

Countering corner healing. Since wall backstabs are fairly consistent many players opt for healing with a corner behind them. It's very difficult to get a BS when your opponent is in this position. Depending on your weapon and your opponents poise kick confirms, rolling attacks and jump attacks can be used to punish the corner heal.

For HMGC mirrors I advocate 2hrR1 (Two-handed rolling attack) when the opponent is NOT healing and 2hJA (Two-handed jump attacks) when they are

For low poise dex/quality builds - double boosted pyromancy, kick->CB or GT spam are effective

Learn which BS options to use in the different situations is key

Backstab Option/InputProsCons
Standard R1 Backstab
  • Simple input
  • Results in a R1 if the backstab misses
Parry Backstab
  • Can be cancelled with a roll, backstep, stance change or toggle
  • The parry animation extends the BS window
  • Locks you in place for the duration of the parry animation
Kick Option Select Backstab
  • Missing the BS results in a kick which is often safer than an R1
  • Input can be tricky when unlocked
Toggle Option Select Backstab
  • The stored toggle will automatically toggle if you get staggered during the BS attempt
  • Not suitable if using a weapon buff
Pyro Option Select Backstab
  • Missing the BS results in a (presumably) Great Combustion cast
