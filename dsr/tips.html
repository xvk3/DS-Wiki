PvP Proficieny

When engaging with an player in PvP you should figure out the following

  • How much poise your opponent has, and consequently how many attack it takes to stagger them etc
  • What utilities do they have? Magic? Pyromancy? 14 Dex? 16 Str? Miracles?
  • What is their ring setup? Are they HavelFap? Dual ring? FaP + Hornet? etc
  • How much latency do you have with your opponent?
  • Do they exhibit any patterns or weaknesses? Constant reverse rolls? Heavy locked strafing? Not holding block? Poor stamina management?
  • Can they counterswap faster than you?
  • Can they toggle escape

Mindgames

Backstabs

My main problem with backstabs was that I was afraid to commit to the unlocked backstab. If my opponent and I were strafing I had the tendency to roll away. Once you overcome this barrier your BS defence and offence improve.

PvP Hotspots

The majority of meta level (120-135) PvP occurs in Oolacile Township, however, the Undead Burg and Darkroot are visited infrequently.

If you're not big into cheating (NoDead / Undead) then arena is a fun and quick way to get PvP with a friend.
