Inventory Tips

Organise your inventory to make the most use of the navigation functionality offered by the triggers (L2/R2). For armour, have the full giant set at the top of your inventory so you can swap to max physical defence from any set using the left trigger (L2), have a similar setup for light armour.

PvP Hotspots

The majority of meta level (120-135) PvP occurs in Oolacile Township, however, the Undead Burg and Darkroot are visited infrequently.

If you're not big into cheating (NoDead / Undead) then arena is a fun and quick way to get PvP with a friend.
